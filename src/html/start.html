Palladio is a web-based platform for the visualization of complex, multi-dimensional data. Visit Humanities+Design to learn more about this NEH funded project. And please send us your feedback!

What can I do with Palladio?

Copy and paste out of your spreadsheets, upload tabular data (e.g. .csv, .tab, .tsv), or link to a public Google spreadsheet to create a new Palladio project.
